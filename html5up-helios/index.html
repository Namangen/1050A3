About The Cozy Cup

Nestled in the heart of the city, The Cozy Cup is more than just a café — it’s a warm, welcoming hug in the form of coffee and croissants.

Whether you're looking to kickstart your day or find your midday escape, we’re here with good vibes, handcrafted brews, and friendly faces.

Coffee Stories, No Sugar.

Richard

As a serious coffee guy, I’ve been to cafés all over the city—but The Cozy Cup hit different. The barista knew exactly how to pull a perfect shot, and the chill atmosphere gave me space to relax and even catch up on some reading. Easily one of my new favourite spots.

Ankita

From the moment I stepped in, I felt welcomed. The rustic design, the friendly team, and the smell of fresh pastries made me want to stay all day. I got the cappuccino with oat milk—creamy and rich! Can’t wait to come back with friends.

Jasmine

I walked in expecting just another café, but The Cozy Cup felt like home. The soft lighting, the calming music, and the way the staff smiled genuinely—it’s rare to feel that kind of warmth in a busy world. Plus, the chai latte? Absolutely divine.
